Pig Dice Game

Pig Dice is a game of chance where you and your opponent each take turns rolling a single die.

Each turn, a player rolls a single die. If the value of the die is anything other than 1, the players score for that turn goes up by that amount.

A player can stop rolling at any time, and their total value from that turn goes into their total score.

Be careful though - if you roll a 1 then you lose all points for that turn and your turn is over.

Grab a partner, enter your names and hit "Start Game" whenever you're ready to play!
